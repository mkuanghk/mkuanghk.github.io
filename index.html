Mengmeng Kuang

Currently, I am serving as an Applied Researcher at the WeChat Group, Tencent. I obtained an M.Phil. degree in Computer Science from The University of Hong Kong, where I had the privilege of being supervised by Dr. Hing-fung Ting. Under his expert guidance, I honed invaluable knowledge about conducting research and academic writing. Prior to my time at HKU, I received a B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, where I spent my final year under the supervision of Prof. Tiejun Zhao. During that period, I acquired a solid foundation in computer science, which helped cultivate my profound interest in computer science research and the natural language processing field.
